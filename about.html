Best Books of the Year

The Midnight Library

By Matt Haig

A captivating and thought-provoking novel that explores the choices we make in life. In the "Midnight Library," Nora Seed gets the chance to undo her regrets and pursue alternative paths in life.

The Vanishing Half

By Brit Bennett

A powerful story that delves into identity, race, and family. The novel follows the lives of twin sisters, who take vastly different paths after growing up in a small, Black community in the South.

Klara and the Sun

By Kazuo Ishiguro

A moving and philosophical novel that explores the nature of love, intelligence, and human relationships through the perspective of Klara, an artificial intelligence robot designed to assist lonely children.
